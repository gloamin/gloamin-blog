I have always enjoyed the beginning of a project.

The first blank page. The empty directory. The moment before the shape of something has been decided.

There is a freedom in those early stages. Nothing is fixed yet, and every choice feels possible.

Of course, eventually the details arrive: structure, organisation, maintenance, and all the small decisions that turn an idea into something real.

But the beginning remains my favourite part.

A quiet room. A cup of tea. A few lines of code. Something slowly taking form.
