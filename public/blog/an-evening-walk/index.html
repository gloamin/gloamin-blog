An Evening Walk

18 July 2026

There is a particular moment at the edge of evening when the world seems to become quieter.

The sharp edges of the day begin to soften. The roads empty, the light fades, and familiar places take on a different character.

These are the hours I find myself returning to most often. Not quite day, not yet night. A small space between things.

Perhaps that is what draws me to making things on the web as well: the chance to create a small place apart from the noise.
